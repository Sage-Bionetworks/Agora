Create a Synapse Account

In order to access data used in Agora, you will need to create a Synapse account.

What is Synapse?

Data is stored in Synapse, an open source software platform used by data scientists to carry out, track, and communicate research in real time. Sage Bionetworks provides Synapse services free of charge to the scientific community through generous support from various funding sources. The content available through Agora would not be possible without the generous participation of research volunteers and collaborating research investigators.

How to Access the Data

1

Register for a Synapse account and agree to the Synapse terms of use.

2

Agree to acknowledge the portal in publications with the following statement: "The results published here are in whole or in part based on data obtained from Agora, a platform that shares evidence in support of AD target discovery initially developed by the NIA-funded AMP-AD consortium."

3

Submit the required Data Use Certificate.
